About Us

Ambit Pharma Ltd is an emerging pharmaceutical company committed to delivering high-quality, compliant, and value-driven medicines across the UK and European markets.

Founded by a team of experienced pharmaceutical executives with over 100 years of combined expertise in management, regulatory affairs, commercial operations, legal, and other key functions, Ambit Pharma Ltd operates with a strong understanding of international markets and regulatory environments.

Our mission is to contribute meaningfully to healthcare by improving access to safe, effective, and affordable treatments. We strategically focus on niche areas with limited competition, delivering value-added medicines that address unmet medical needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and compliance.

In partnership with Athena Pharmaceutiques SAS, a global organization specializing in high-quality pharmaceutical products designed to improve patient compliance, simplify administration, and enhance overall well-being, we introduce innovative and competitively priced products into the UK market.
