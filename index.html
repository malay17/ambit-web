About Us

Ambit Pharma Ltd is an emerging pharmaceutical company headquartered in the United Kingdom, focused on delivering high-quality, compliant and value-driven medicines across the UK and European markets. Our mission is to contribute meaningfully to healthcare by improving access to safe, effective, and affordable treatments.

The company is founded by a group of experienced pharmaceutical executives with over 100 years of combined expertise across management, regulatory affairs, commercial operations, legal, and other key functional areas of the global pharmaceutical industry. This breadth of experience enables us to operate with a strong understanding of international markets and regulatory environments.

Ambit Pharma Ltd has established a strategic partnership with Athena Pharmaceutiques SAS, a global organisation specialising in the development of high-quality pharmaceutical products designed to improve patient compliance, simplify administration, and enhance overall well-being. This collaboration supports the introduction of innovative and competitively priced products into the UK market.
